# nTZS Sandbox — Participant Disclosure & Consent
# Ufafanuzi na Ridhaa ya Mshiriki — Majaribio ya nTZS

**Sandbox Ref:** LD.170/515/02/1254 · **Prepared:** 8 July 2026
*(Testing Parameters 12, 13, R-3 — disclosures in English and Kiswahili.)*

---

## ENGLISH

**What nTZS is.** nTZS is a digital token pegged **1:1 to the Tanzanian Shilling (TZS)** and backed by
TZS reserves held in a ring-fenced trust account at a licensed bank. You can hold, send, and redeem it
for TZS.

**This is a regulatory sandbox test.** nTZS is being tested under the Bank of Tanzania Fintech
Regulatory Sandbox (Ref. LD.170/515/02/1254). **nTZS is NOT legal tender** and its use is **restricted
to approved test participants**. This is a limited pilot, not a fully licensed product.

**Your money is in Shillings.** All balances, receipts, and payouts are shown and settled in **TZS**.
You can redeem your nTZS back to TZS to your mobile-money account at any time, subject to the limits below.

**Test limits (per participant).**
- Up to **1,000,000 TZS** per transaction.
- Up to **2,000,000 TZS** per day.
- Up to **60,000,000 TZS** per month.

**Fees & timing.** Any fees and the expected redemption time are shown to you before you confirm a
transaction. Mobile-money settlement times may vary with the network.

**Risks.** This is pilot software. Although nTZS is fully reserve-backed 1:1 and every issuance happens
only after your cash is received, you should understand that this is a test environment and you should
only transact amounts you are comfortable using in a pilot.

**Your identity.** Every wallet is linked to a verified identity — no anonymous wallets. You must
complete identity verification (KYC) to transact.

**Data protection.** Your personal data is handled in line with the Personal Data Protection Act, 2022,
and will be securely deleted at the end of the test unless the law requires otherwise.

**Complaints.** Contact **support@ntzs.co.tz**. We aim to resolve complaints within **5 business days**.
You may escalate unresolved matters to the Bank of Tanzania.

**Consent.** By continuing, you confirm you have read and understood this disclosure and agree to
participate in the nTZS sandbox test.

## KISWAHILI

**nTZS ni nini.** nTZS ni sarafu ya kidijitali iliyothaminishwa **sawa na Shilingi ya Tanzania (TZS) kwa
uwiano wa 1:1**, ikidhaminiwa na akiba ya TZS iliyohifadhiwa katika akaunti maalum ya dhamana (trust)
katika benki iliyosajiliwa. Unaweza kuishikilia, kuituma, na kuibadilisha kuwa TZS.

**Haya ni majaribio ya kidhibiti (sandbox).** nTZS inajaribiwa chini ya Mfumo wa Majaribio wa Kifintech
wa Benki Kuu ya Tanzania (Kumb. LD.170/515/02/1254). **nTZS SIYO fedha halali (legal tender)** na
matumizi yake **yamewekewa mipaka kwa washiriki walioidhinishwa** wa majaribio. Haya ni majaribio ya
kiwango kidogo, siyo bidhaa iliyopewa leseni kamili.

**Fedha yako iko katika Shilingi.** Salio, risiti, na malipo yote yanaonyeshwa na kulipwa kwa **TZS**.
Unaweza kubadilisha nTZS yako kuwa TZS kwenda akaunti yako ya simu wakati wowote, kwa kuzingatia mipaka
iliyo hapa chini.

**Mipaka ya majaribio (kwa kila mshiriki).**
- Hadi **TZS 1,000,000** kwa kila muamala.
- Hadi **TZS 2,000,000** kwa siku.
- Hadi **TZS 60,000,000** kwa mwezi.

**Ada na muda.** Ada yoyote na muda unaotarajiwa wa kubadilisha fedha huonyeshwa kabla ya kuthibitisha
muamala. Muda wa malipo ya fedha za simu unaweza kutofautiana kulingana na mtandao.

**Hatari.** Hii ni programu ya majaribio. Ingawa nTZS imedhaminiwa kikamilifu kwa uwiano wa 1:1 na kila
utoaji hufanyika tu baada ya fedha yako kupokelewa, tafadhali elewa kuwa hili ni mazingira ya majaribio
na utumie kiasi ambacho uko tayari kukitumia katika majaribio.

**Utambulisho wako.** Kila pochi (wallet) imeunganishwa na utambulisho uliothibitishwa — hakuna pochi
zisizojulikana. Ni lazima ukamilishe uthibitisho wa utambulisho (KYC) ili kufanya miamala.

**Ulinzi wa taarifa.** Taarifa zako binafsi zitashughulikiwa kwa mujibu wa Sheria ya Ulinzi wa Taarifa
Binafsi ya mwaka 2022, na zitafutwa kwa usalama mwishoni mwa majaribio isipokuwa sheria itahitaji
vinginevyo.

**Malalamiko.** Wasiliana kupitia **support@ntzs.co.tz**. Tunalenga kutatua malalamiko ndani ya **siku
5 za kazi**. Unaweza kupeleka malalamiko yasiyotatuliwa kwa Benki Kuu ya Tanzania.

**Ridhaa.** Kwa kuendelea, unathibitisha kuwa umesoma na kuelewa ufafanuzi huu na unakubali kushiriki
katika majaribio ya nTZS.
